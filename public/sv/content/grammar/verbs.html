Verbs

Overview

Verbs inflect for the present and past tense and for mood, but not for person or number.

Click here for a list of common irregular verbs.

Conjugation

Conjugation varies depending on the verbs group, of which Swedish has 4.

Verb Stem

The verb stem is used as a basis of conjugating a given verb and is based off of the present tense of the verb.

  • If the present tense ends in -ar, the -r is removed e.g., kallar → kalla-.
  • If the present tense ends in -er, the -er is removed e.g., stänger → stäng-.
  • For short verbs, the -r is removed e.g., syr → sy-.

Note that the imperative is the same as the stem.

Verb Groups

Group Description Infinitive Present Passive Past Supine Past Participle (com/neut/pl)
1 Regular -ar verbs. (stem) (stem) + r (tense) + s* (stem) + de (stem) + t (stem) + d/t/de
2a Regular -er verbs ending in a voiced consonant. (stem) + a (stem) + er (stem) + de (stem) + t (stem) + d/t/da
2b Regular -er verbs ending in a voiceless consonant. (stem) + a (stem) + er (stem) + te (stem) + t (stem) + t/t/ta
3 Short verbs, ending in -r. (stem) (stem) + r (stem) + dde (stem) + tt (stem) + dd/tt/dda
4 Strong and irregular verbs, ending in -er or -r. (stem) + a (stem) + er/r (stem) + ?** (stem) + it (stem) + en/et/na

  • (*) For verbs ending in -r, the -r is replaced by the -s altogether. Verbs ending in -er often lose the -e- as well. Monosyllabic and verbs already ending in -s are exceptions.
  • (**) The stem vowel changes for strong verbs, often following a definite pattern.

Tenses

Examples of the tenses are shown below.

Tense English Swedish
Infinitive to work arbeta
to walk gå
Present I work jag arbetar
I walk jag går
Past imperfect I worked jag arbetade
I walked jag gick
Past perfect I have worked jag har arbetat
I have walked jag har gått

Past Tenses

There are multiple different was a senetence may be phrased in the past tense:

English Swedish
Simple past I ate the dinner jag åt maten
Composite past (using supine) I have eaten the dinner jag har ätit maten
Past participle common The dinner is eaten maten är äten
Past participle neuter The apple is eaten äpplet är ätet
Past participle plural The apples are eaten äpplena är ätna
