Irregular Comparative/Superlative Adjectives

Variations in the superlative form: adjectives with the endings -er have the superlative ending -errimus

comparative superlative
miser miserable miserior miserrimus
pulcher beautiful pulchrior pulcherrimus

Other Irregulars:
